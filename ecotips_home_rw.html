Reduce Waste

Waste reduction strategies are important for businesses and homes to minimise how much rubbish you produce. Introducing a set of techniques to reduce waste helps ensure as little refuse as possible goes to landfill.

Compost Food Scraps

Ideally, we would eat all the food we buy. If we can’t eat it, or give it away, or feed it to our chickens, then wasted food belongs in a compost heap of some sort.

Purchase Second-Hand

Donating unwanted items is another easy way to reduce your own waste. Plus, it’s normally significantly cheaper to buy second-hand.

Only Buy What You Need

The easiest way to reduce waste is to avoid buying extra stuff you won’t use in the first place.
