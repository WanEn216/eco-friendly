Conserve Water

If we all do our part in saving precious water supplies, we can make a huge difference for the environment. (Try pressing the image for a while?)

In Bathroom

To kick off, turn the tap off while brushing your teeth since a running tap can waste more than 6 litres of water a minute! Other than that, get a cistern displacement device to save up to 5,000 litres of water every year. They are free from most water companies. Moreover, shower instead of bathe. An average bath uses around 80 litres of water, but a shower typically uses between 6 and 45 litres.

In Garden

Sprinklers can use as much as 1,000 litres of water an hour! In truth, it’s okay for the lawn to go brown, it will recover the next time it rains. Apart from that, use a water butt to catch large amounts of rainwater and use this to water your plants, clean your car and wash your windows. Some tips here, using mulch and bark in your garden, it will help to reduce evaporation by up to 75%.

In Kitchen

Do you know putting a large bottle of tap water in the fridge to save waiting for the tap to run cold. Waiting for the tap to run cold can waste 10 litres of water a day! Besides, you are encouraged to only fill the kettle with the amount of water needed. Don’t forget to put lids on saucepans to reduce the amount of water lost during heating. Furthermore, use a washing-up bowl in your sinks to reduce the volume of water you use to fill the area.
