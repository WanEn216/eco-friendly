Reduce

Making less waste to begin with means there’s less waste to clean up.

Here are some easy ways you can reduce the amount of waste you make:

1. Pack your lunch in a lunchbox

Paper and plastic bags create a huge amount of waste since they need to take hundreds of years to decompose.

  • Try a durable lunchbox or reusable lunch bag instead.

2. Bring reusable bags to the grocery store

Most stores sell canvas or durable plastic shopping bags that can be used again and again. Some grocery stores even give you a little money off at the checkstand when you bring your own bags.

3. Say no to bottled water

  • Instead of buying bottled water, use a reusable water bottle.

4. Watch what you buy

When you’re in the supermarket, pay attention to the packaging. Can the container be cleaned out and used for something else?

And when comparing two similar products, if one has less packaging, consider making that your selection.
