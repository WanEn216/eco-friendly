Transportation

We are encouraged to reduce driving to help minimize our carbon footprint and protect the environment. (There are four interesting things in the image!)

Walking - Adopting pedestrian-friendly sidewalks and dedicated zones for non-motorized transport is crucial for promoting walking as a mode of transportation.

Cycling - Some cities have encouraged people to use bikes for short trips, which not only cuts down emissions but also promotes a healthy lifestyle.

Public - These vehicles significantly reduce pollution and offer a more sustainable solution for mass transportation.

Electric Vehicles - Electric Vehicles (EVs) run on electricity, reducing reliance on fossil fuels and producing zero tailpipe emissions.
